Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or passwords with anyone.
2. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links.
3. Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks.
4. Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown sources.
5. Enable two-factor authentication on all accounts.
6. Regularly monitor your bank transactions.
7. Use official apps and avoid third-party payment links.
8. Do not approve collect requests unless you trust the sender.

Financial Safety Tips:
9. Remember that you do not need to enter your PIN to receive money.
10. Immediately report fraud on cybercrime.gov.in or call helpline 1930.
