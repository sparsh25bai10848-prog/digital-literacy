Social Media Do's and Don'ts

Do's:
1. Use strong and unique passwords for your accounts.
2. Keep your profile professional and appropriate.
3. Verify information before sharing it online.
4. Respect others and communicate politely.
5. Use privacy settings to control who can see your content.

Don'ts:
1. Do not share personal information like phone number or address publicly.
2. Avoid posting inappropriate or offensive content.
3. Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
4. Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown messages.
5. Do not spread misinformation or fake news.
